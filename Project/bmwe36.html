BMW e36 M3

$35 000

The third generation of the BMW 3 Series
Produced from 1992 to 2000

Body styles

The initial models were of the four-door sedan body style,
followed by the coupe, convertible, wagon ("Touring"), hatchback
("Compact") and the rare four-door convertible Baur TC4 in later years.

Compared

The E36 was the first 3 Series to be offered in a hatchback body style.
It was also the first 3 Series to be available with a six-speed manual
transmission (in the 1996 M3), a five-speed automatic transmission and
a four-cylinder diesel engine. The multi-link rear suspension was also
a significant upgrade as compared to the previous generations of the 3 Series.

Engine

The high performance E36 M3 is powered by the BMW S50 or BMW S52
straight-six engine (depending on country). The E36 M3 was introduced
